The Nation Builders.

UpConstructionis a major Indian technology, engineering, construction, manufacturing and financial services conglomerate, with global operations. UpConstruction addresses critical needs in key sectors - Hydrocarbon, Infrastructure, Power, Process Industries and Defence - for customers in over 30 countries around the world. UpConstruction is engaged in core high impact sectors of the economy and our integrated capabilities span the entire spectrum of 'design to deliver'. With over 8 decades of a strong, customer focused approach and a continuous quest for world-class quality, we have unmatched expertise across Technology, Engineering, Construction, Infrastructure Projects and Manufacturing.

Office Spaces.

We have the capability to design-and-build world-class IT infrastructure – software development blocks, IT & software parks, techno & business parks and built-to-suit IT offices ready for people to come ‘plug-and-play’.

Bridges.

Having pioneered the design and construction of major bridges, we have a track record of having built some of the finest bridges both in India and overseas using ingenious, cutting-edge bridge construction techniques like incremental launching, segmental construction, cable stay, precast & pre-stressed concrete and steel & concrete composite construction.

Defence Installations.

Backed by the expertise and experience gained from managing mega projects for over eight decades, we have established our pre-eminence in shoring up the country's defences with the capability to build a variety of critical defence establishments.

Housing.

Over the past couple of decades, our Buildings business vertical has been creating high-end luxury homes for some of the most prestigious communities by bringing to play our expertise in Design & Build, using quality materials and equipping the edifices with ultra-modern home automation systems.

The Henning Holck-Larsen(HHL) centre

  • Constructed to commemorate the 60th anniversary of UpConstruction.
  • The uniquely shaped corporate museum – the Henning Holck-Larsen (HHL) Centre – is dedicated to the memory of one of UpConstruction's founding fathers and Chairman Emeritus.
  • Recently re-designed and re-appointed, the HHL Centre is perhaps the best example of the digital transformation witnessed across the organization.

In an entirely digital, touch-enabled environment, visitors from across the board can experience the organization's 8-decade-long history and its width of capabilities.
